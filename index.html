About

Hi,

I am a UX designer focusing on animation, interactivity, and empathy. My goal is to have a real, positive effect on individuals and society by helping to solve problems and design solutions.

I’ve worked in production for advertising with creative agencies and my artistic background is in improv and experimental animation.

I’m a lover of hip-hop, jazz, climbing, basketball, film, and motorcycle rides.

UX Skills

Animation + Interaction Design

I love to add animation and interaction design to apps, websites, and other projects. With a background in animation, I love to see how the user can be delighted and engaged by motion and interactivity.

User Empathy

In order to empathize with the user, I create user personas, empathy maps, user journeys, and storyboards of the user’s experience. I strive to understand the user's pain points and motivations as much as possible to create the best experience the user can have.

User Research

To understand user’s needs I employ preliminary user research through interviews, surveys, and field studies. Throughout this process, I also conduct user testing and usability fine-tuning.
